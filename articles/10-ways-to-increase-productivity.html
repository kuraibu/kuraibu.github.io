10 Ways To Increase Your Productivity

  1. Don't Over Plan

    Web technologies are ever evolving and if you do not take the time as a developer to hone your skills, you will soon find yourself trailing behind.

    Take the time to learn new technologies and even new ways of doing things in the technologies you already use.

    Aim to end every day being better than you were yesterday.

  5. Don't Re-Invent the Wheel

    At times, you may find that you have bits of code that you reuse all the time in your work. There is no reason to start from scratch evrytime you use the same piece of code.

    Get in the habit of keeping a library of code snippets. Over time you will notice your development process getting a lot faster.

  6. Ask For Help

    There is an amazing community of designers on the internet. If you get stuck on any piece of code, just find yourself online and pose a question to the community on stackoverflow or github forums (or whereever else...) and there is sure to be someone that can help you solve your problem.
